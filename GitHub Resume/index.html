LED Kinetic Energy Harvester

Building an OpAmp based circuit on a breadboard with an NS555 Real Time Clock interfacing with a Piezo motion sensor. In conjunction with the Real Time Clock, our OpAmp uses an inductor and capacitor to form a DC/DC boost converter. The boost converter "boosts" the small signal sent from the Piezo motion sensor to our LED; which in turn lights up as it's flicked.
